How to make Tea

At the very least, tea is a flavourful way of getting fluid into your body each day. On top of that, studies have shown teas can help protect your teeth and your heart.

Ingredients

  • Tea
  • Water
  • Milk - Dairy/Plant-based (Optional)
  • Suagr/Honey (Optional)

Preparation

  1. Run the tap a little so the water's nicely aerated, and only boil it once to keep the oxygen level up. Oxygen in water helps flavour!
  2. Pop a tea bag into your mug(always a bigi mug)
  3. Pour the hot water over the tea bag and stir briefly.
  4. Tea needs time to unlock all it flavour, so give it 3-4 minutes to do its thing. This is a perfect to grab a sneaky cookie or daydream about vacations.
  5. Before removing the tea bag, gently squish it with a spoon against the side of the mug. Not too much or you'll make it bitter.
  6. If you want, throw in some milk or sugar or nothing else at all.
  7. Enjoy!
